## Guidelines for Shorts (30-60 seconds)
1. Hook in first 3 seconds - create curiosity or controversy
2. Each segment should be 5-8 seconds
3. End with clear CTA
4. Use simple language and punchy statements
5. Include text overlays for key points

## Guidelines for Long-form (6-10 minutes)
1. Start with the myth and why people believe it
2. Present evidence systematically
3. Use analogies to explain complex concepts
4. Address counterarguments
5. End with clear takeaway and related topics

## Tone Guidelines
- Be confident but not condescending
- Show empathy for why people believe myths
- Use humor when appropriate
- Be educational, not preachy
- Make complex topics accessible
